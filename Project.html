Empowering Communities: Three Tales of Impact from East Africa

In Ethiopia, Lana Reese Preschool celebrates a significant milestone as the hard-earned title deed is now secured by our partner, Harvest Church of God. With new water wells and latrines, the school's infrastructure has been fortified, providing a more conducive learning environment. The innovative initiative to sell clean water ensures sustainability, with proceeds supporting dedicated educators. Meanwhile, Acheber Town's preschool, operational for two years, receives the esteemed designation of the top preschool in the South-Western Shoa Zone, a testament to the transformative impact of your support. In Kisumu, Kenya, Purpose Driven School expresses deep gratitude for the purchase of 68 treated mosquito bed nets, a vital step in combating malaria, a prevalent threat to African children and expectant mothers.

Edoy Water Project Triumph: A Milestone Achieved for Health and Community

We're overjoyed to announce the successful completion of the Edoy Water Project! Thanks to your generous support, women and children in five communities are now accessing clean water from newly built distribution centers near their homes. This transformative initiative significantly improves community health by eliminating the need to collect contaminated water from rivers, preventing water-borne diseases.
